# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback, and keep discussion focused on the project. Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, doxxing, sustained disruption, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior is harmful or disruptive. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion; retaliation against reporters is prohibited.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project elsewhere.
